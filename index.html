About

What is a Hackathon? Hackathon is usually a 24h or 48h event where programmers come together for a short period of time to collaborate on a project. As hackathon grows in popularity, many variants of hackathon have popped up, some even without coding.

What is a MedHack2021? MedHack2021 is a virtual hackathon with the theme of Digitalised Medicine. Participants will come together (virtually) over a weekend to create technological solutions that solve any healthcare-related problem. The range of solutions is pretty much endless, ranging from enhancing medical education with VR, to assisting image diagnostic with AI, from improving communications in the hospital, to finding the optimal vaccine distribution algorithms in the pandemic.

Can I take part? Students from any universities in the UK are welcome to attend. No experience in medicine, technology, or programming is needed. Just bring your enthusiasm and creativity. Teams consist of 2 to 5 people.
